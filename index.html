OASIS

Organization for Action in Sustainability and Innovation Society

A USD student collective bridging environmental awareness with hands-on action through engineering, sustainability practices, creative expression, and community building. We empower students to become environmental changemakers locally and beyond.

About Us

Our Mission

To cultivate a culture of sustainability at USD through transformative experiences that connect students with the ocean, land, and regenerative technologies, turning environmental awareness into community action.

Our Vision

To be the leading student-led environmental innovation hub in San Diego, where every USD student has access to meaningful experiences that empower them as agents of environmental change in their communities.

More Information

What OASIS Is

A student collective focused on environmental sustainability and climate action. A community that connects students with nature and eco-conscious practices. An experience laboratory from whale watching to sustainable technology. A bridge between environmental awareness and tangible action.

What OASIS Is NOT

Just a traditional recycling or composting club. A purely academic/theoretical organization. Exclusive to environmental science students. A confrontational activist group. Just occasional events without continuity.

Our Projects

Whale watching expeditions in La Paz, surf sessions and beach cleanups, campus composting systems, digital sustainability campaigns, Biotron plant-music installations using Playtronica technology, bio-design workshops, alumni mentorship programs, and sustainable materials research.

Join Us

OASIS welcomes students from all majors and backgrounds. Whether you're passionate about engineering, art, community building, or simply curious about sustainability, there's a place for you in our oasis. Together, we're creating a greener, more innovative future for USD and beyond.
