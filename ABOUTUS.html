About Us

Our Mission

To cultivate a culture of sustainability at USD through transformative experiences that connect students with the ocean, land, and regenerative technologies, turning environmental awareness into community action.

Our Vision

To be the leading student-led environmental innovation hub in San Diego, where every USD student has access to meaningful experiences that empower them as agents of environmental change.
